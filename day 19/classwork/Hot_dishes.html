N1:

Mwvadi(cooked pork)

This dish is very famous locally. Everyone enjoys it, but in restaurant IBERIA, we serve it differently, which introduces

our customers to a completely new flavour. Special topping that goes with Mwvadi, is wild pomegranate juice.

PPP(Price Per Portion)-29Lari

N2:

Khinkali

Khinkali is the most popular national dish in Georgia. Everywhere you go, and ask for the tastiest national dish, you

will for sure be offered a plate of khinkali. It for sure is called the "perfect dish" by many people locally. (DO NOT eat it with a fork or Goergians will get mad at you.)

PPP(Price Per Portion)- 4.5 Lari (3pcs)

N2:

Khawapuri

Khawapuri is the second most popular national dish. It is also approved and liked by many tourists. you can not go

wrong with Khawapuri and some of our in-restaurant made wine (check the drinks section for more information about wine.).

PPP(Price Per Portion)- 16 Lari
